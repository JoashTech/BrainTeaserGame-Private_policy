1 Introduction

JoshTechApps (“we,” “our,” or “us”) develops mobile quiz games, including Brain Teaser Game and Football Quiz Game (collectively, “the Games”). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use any of our Games.

📊 Data Flow Transparency

Our Role: We enable third-party services that may collect anonymized data to provide analytics, advertising, and authentication services.

Their Role: Third-party services independently collect and process device data according to their own privacy policies.

Your Data: Game progress, statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store personal data beyond Firebase services.

2 Data We Collect

In simple terms: We don't collect your name, email, address, or any personal information. We don't know who you are.

What we do collect (anonymously):

  • Device ID (Advertising ID) – A random ID from your phone that ad networks use to show relevant ads
  • Lifetime Coins Earned – Total coins you have earned over your entire playtime (used for leaderboard rankings)
  • Game Stats – Total games played, perfect levels, current streak, longest streak, achievements
  • Country – If you choose to set it, for country‑specific leaderboards
  • Player Name – Only what you choose to call yourself (like “BRAIN_MASTER” or “FOOTBALL_MASTER”)
  • Profile Picture – If you upload one (stored as Base64 on your device and in Firestore for leaderboard display)

All this data is anonymous. It cannot be traced back to you as an individual.

3 How We Use Your Information

  • 🆔 To Create Your Anonymous Account – Firebase Authentication creates a unique ID to identify you in leaderboards
  • 🏆 To Display Leaderboards – Global, weekly, monthly, and country rankings based on lifetime coins earned (spending coins or coins purchases are considered on your rank)
  • 👤 To Show Player Profiles – Your chosen name, country, profile picture, title, and stats are visible to other players on leaderboards
  • 📸 To Store Profile Pictures – If you upload a profile picture, it is stored locally and synced to Firebase so other players can see it
  • 💾 To Save Game Progress – Levels completed, stars earned, and achievements are stored locally
  • 💰 To Track Virtual Economy – Current coin balance (local) and lifetime earnings (Firebase) are maintained
  • 📊 For Analytics – Firebase Analytics helps us understand how users interact with the Games
  • 📺 To Serve Advertisements – Ad networks use device data to show relevant ads and enable rewarded videos
  • 🔧 To Improve the Games – Crash reports from Firebase Crashlytics help us fix bugs

6 Virtual Economy (Coins & Lives)

🪙 Important Information About Virtual Items

  • Coins and lives are virtual items with no real-world value
  • They cannot be exchanged for cash, goods, or services
  • All coin transactions are tracked locally and synced to Firebase for leaderboard rankings
  • Purchased coins are non-refundable except as required by applicable law
  • We reserve the right to modify, manage, or eliminate virtual items at any time

How Coins Are Tracked:

  • Current Balance: Stored locally, shown in‑game, used for spending – this is your wallet
  • Lifetime Earnings: Synced to Firebase, used for leaderboard rankings – this never decreases, even if you spend coins
  • Coin Sources: Correct answers , perfect level bonus , daily challenge , share rewards , daily login , achievements

7 Data Storage & Security

💾 Where Your Data Lives

Local Storage (Your Device)

  • Game progress (levels completed, stars earned)
  • Player name and profile picture (Base64)
  • Current coin balance and transaction history
  • Game settings (sound, music, notifications)
  • Daily challenge status and streak

Firebase Servers (Google Cloud)

  • Anonymous User ID (Firebase UID)
  • Lifetime coins earned (for leaderboards)
  • Total games played, perfect levels, streaks
  • Player name and country (for leaderboard display)
  • Profile picture (Base64, for leaderboard display)
  • Last updated timestamp

Ad Network Servers

  • Advertising ID and device information
  • Ad impressions and interactions
  • Rewarded ad completion status

Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security practices. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9 Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • 🔍 Right to Access - Request a copy of your data stored on Firebase
  • 🗑️ Right to Deletion - Request deletion of your Firebase data
  • ⚙️ Right to Opt-Out - Opt out of personalized advertising
  • 📤 Right to Data Portability - Export your game data
  • 🛑 Right to Object - Object to data processing for legitimate interests
  • 🖼️ Remove Profile Picture – You can change or delete your profile picture at any time from the Account screen

How to Exercise Your Rights:

  • Reset Progress: Use the "Reset Progress" button in Account & More settings to delete all local and Firebase data
  • Email Us: contactjoshtech@gmail.com for data access or deletion requests
  • Opt Out of Ads: Device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of personalized ads
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the Games removes all local data; Firebase data will be cleared after 120 days of inactivity

10 International Data Transfers

The Games are hosted and operated using Firebase services (Google Cloud) with servers located in the United States (nam5). If you use the Games from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

By using the Games, you consent to this transfer of your data to the United States. We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your data is protected according to this Privacy Policy.
