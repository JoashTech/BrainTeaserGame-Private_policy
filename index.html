1 Introduction

JoshTechApps ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game application, Brain Teaser Game (the "Game").

üìä Data Flow Transparency

Our Role: We enable third-party services that may collect anonymized data to provide analytics, advertising, and authentication services.

Their Role: Third-party services independently collect and process device data according to their own privacy policies.

Your Data: Game progress, statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store personal data.

2 Data We Collect

In simple terms: We don't collect your name, email, address, or any personal information. We don't know who you are.

What we do collect (anonymously):

  • Device ID (Advertising ID) - A random ID from your phone that ad networks use to show relevant ads
  • Game Stats - Your lifetime coins, games played, and achievements (for leaderboards)
  • Country - If you choose to set it, for country-specific leaderboards
  • Player Name - Only what you choose to call yourself (like "BRAIN_MASTER")

All this data is anonymous. It cannot be traced back to you as an individual.

6 Virtual Economy (Coins & Lives)

ü™ô Important Information About Virtual Items

  • Coins and lives are virtual items with no real-world value
  • They cannot be exchanged for cash, goods, or services
  • All coin transactions are tracked locally and synced to Firebase for leaderboard rankings
  • Purchased coins are non-refundable except as required by applicable law
  • We reserve the right to modify, manage, or eliminate virtual items at any time

How Coins Are Tracked:

  • Current Balance: Stored locally, shown in-game, used for spending
  • Lifetime Earnings: Synced to Firebase, used for leaderboard rankings
  • Coin Sources: Correct answers, level completion, daily challenge, share rewards, daily bonus, achievements
